# CES Public Materials

Public research materials from Jon Smirl's CES (Constant Elasticity of Substitution) framework research.

## Contents

### Lunar Gravity Power Plant
A 52-page concept paper describing a gravity power plant that generates GW-scale electricity from asteroid material descending a superconducting cable from Earth-Moon L1 to the lunar surface. The same rock that generates the power becomes structural steel and radiation shielding for rotating habitats. One 600m asteroid yields one 2 km habitat for 200,000 people.

- **Paper:** [Lunar_Gravity_Power_Plant.pdf](lunar_gravity_power_plant/Lunar_Gravity_Power_Plant.pdf)
- **Zenodo DOI:** [10.5281/zenodo.19188043](https://zenodo.org/record/19188043)

### HEA CES Predictions
Application of the CES framework to High Entropy Alloy (HEA) property prediction. Includes theory, computational scripts, and experimental proposals.

- **Paper:** [HEA_CES_Predictions.pdf](hea_ces_predictions/HEA_CES_Predictions.pdf)

### Superconductor DFT
DFT (Density Functional Theory) calculations on superconductor candidates, primarily MgB₂H₂ and related compounds. GPU-accelerated Quantum ESPRESSO calculations for electron-phonon coupling.

- Key result: MgB₂H₂ λ_optic = 1.70, T_c = 93-185K (McMillan to strong-coupling)
- CDW detected at 3/8 q-points
